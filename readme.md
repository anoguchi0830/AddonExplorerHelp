Express Helper is a lightweight discovery companion for Adobe Express that helps Japanese and Korean users find add-ons more easily.

While the Adobe Express marketplace provides a wide range of powerful add-ons, many listings are primarily described in English. This can make it difficult for users who search using Japanese or Korean keywords to discover relevant add-ons.

Express Helper improves discoverability by providing a searchable catalog of Adobe Express add-ons with localized metadata. Users can browse categories, search in their own language, and quickly open the corresponding add-on page in the official Adobe Express marketplace.

Key capabilities include:

• Search add-ons using Japanese or Korean keywords
• Browse add-ons by category
• View localized add-on descriptions
• Open the official Adobe Express add-on page directly from the panel
• Lightweight panel interface designed for quick discovery

Express Helper is designed to complement the Adobe Express ecosystem by making it easier for users to explore available add-ons in their preferred language.
